Hello Democracy Prep friends and supporters,

Welcome to the start of another exciting school year. Ten years ago we were approved to open our first school, Democracy Prep Charter Middle School. Since that first 6th grade class, we have grown to educate more than 5,000 scholars in four regions at 17 schools and one program! In celebration of our 10th anniversary we have started the Democracy Prep Blog to better share the amazing stories and achievements of our scholars and teachers. Click here or go to blog.democracyprep.org. Be sure to bookmark it!
